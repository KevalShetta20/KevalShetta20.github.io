The title of my project

BuddiesGram

BuddiesGram is a free photo and video sharing app . People can upload photos or videos to our service and share them with their followers or with a select group of friends. They can also view, comment and like posts shared by their friends on Instagram.

It's a fast, simple, and convenient way for family and friends to chat, create group texts, share photos and videos, send and receive documents, and engage in private, secure conversations anytime, day or night.

It is basically a combination of Instagram and WhatsApp
